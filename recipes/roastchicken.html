Roast Chicken Recipe



Begin by taking a whole preferably free range farm chicken and placing it on it's back in an oven ready platter. Place 100 grams of butter inside the chicken along with some rock salt and a lemon that you cut slits into.

Sprinkle some salt onto the chicken and sprinkle over the surface of the chicken some olive oil and herbs. Then place the chicken into the oven at 180 degrees celsius and leave it there for 40 minutes, after 40 minutes pull it out and with a spoon take the oil at the bottom of the platter and pour it over the chicken. Place the chicken back into the oven. Repeat this process two more times and your roast chicken will be ready.
